### **Community & Socials**

Note that team members will never DM you first or ask for your seed phrase.

- **X (Twitter):** [x.com/ernapp](https://x.com/ernapp) – _Official announcements and updates._

- **Telegram:** [t.me/ernofficial](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://t.me/ernofficial) – _Community chat and support._


### **Technical & Security**

For developers, auditors, and data analysts verifying the protocol.

- **GitHub:** [github.com/ernorg/ern](https://github.com/ernorg/ern) – _Open source code and smart contracts._

- **Audits:** [ernorg/ern/audits](https://github.com/ernorg/ern/tree/main/audits) – _Full security reports and verification._

- **Dune Analytics:** [dune.com/ern/ern](https://dune.com/ern/ern) – _Track Protocol TVL, Yield, and Payouts._


* * *

#### **Safety Reminders**

Ern does not have a governance token. Beware of "Airdrop" scams claiming otherwise.

- Check URLs: Scammers often buy ads on Google. Always double-check the URL bar matches `ern.app`.

- Support: We will never ask you to "validate your wallet" or input your private key on a random website.
