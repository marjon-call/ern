Ern prioritizes security by operating as a fully **non-custodial protocol**. This means the Ern team **never holds or controls your funds**; instead, your assets are managed entirely by **open-source smart contracts** on the Ethereum blockchain. These contracts have undergone **rigorous external security audits** to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities, ensuring that your deposits and withdrawals are executed programmatically without human intervention or centralized risk.
