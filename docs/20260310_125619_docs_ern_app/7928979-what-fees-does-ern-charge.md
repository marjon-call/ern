- Protocol Fee: 10% of generated yield

- Deposit Fee: 0%

- Withdrawal Fee: 0%

- Early Withdrawal Fee — 0.1%

  - Applies only if funds are withdrawn during the first 48 hours after deposit.

  - Once the 48-hour window passes, withdrawals are completely free.
