**No.** You can withdraw your principal and your earnings at any time. There are no "unbonding" periods or withdrawal queues.

However, and to prevent protocol abuse, during the first 48 hours of the initial deposit a 0.1% withdraw fee applies. After 48 hours, withdrawals are completely fee-free.
