Safety on Ern is grounded in its **non-custodial architecture**, meaning the Ern team **never holds or has access to your funds**. Instead, your assets are locked in **open-source smart contracts** that operate strictly according to their code, which has been verified through **external security audits**. This ensures that no central authority can mismanage, freeze, or steal your deposits, giving you full control over your assets at all times via your private keys.

To generate yield, the protocol interacts exclusively with **battle-tested lending markets** on the **Ethereum Mainnet**, chosen for their extensive track records and **deep liquidity**.

While these measures significantly reduce exposure to common failures, it is important to understand that interacting with any Decentralized Finance (DeFi) protocol involves **inherent risks**. These include, but are not limited to, **smart contract vulnerabilities**, **market volatility**, and **potential de-pegging events** of underlying assets. By connecting your wallet and interacting with the Ern application, you explicitly acknowledge these risks and agree to Ern's Terms and Conditions. We encourage all users to review these terms and our audit reports thoroughly before depositing funds.
