Ern operates on the Ethereum Mainnet. wBTC (Wrapped Bitcoin) is the standard ERC-20 representation of Bitcoin, allowing it to be held in your Ethereum wallet (like MetaMask). You can bridge wBTC to native Bitcoin using various third-party bridges or CEXs at any time.
