To check your balance, you must first **connect your Ethereum-compatible wallet** (such as MetaMask or Rabby) to the **Ern application dashboard**.

Once connected, the interface retrieves on-chain data to display your **current stablecoin deposit** and your **total accrued Bitcoin (wBTC) earnings** in real-time. This dashboard acts as a window into the smart contract, showing you exactly how much capital you have staked and how much yield has been generated since your last withdrawal.
