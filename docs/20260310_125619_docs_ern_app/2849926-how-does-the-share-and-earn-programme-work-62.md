**Share & Earn** Ern’s referral programme allows you to earn a portion of the protocol’s fees by inviting others to use Ern. For every user you refer, **you receive 10% of the yield they generate**, paid directly by the protocol.

There is **no cap** on referrals or earnings.

* * *

### **How it works**

1. **Generate your referral link** Open the Ern app and select **Share & Earn** in the navigation. Your unique referral link will be generated automatically.

2. **Share your link** Send your referral link to anyone. There are no restrictions on who you can refer.

3. **Earn 10% of their yield** When a referred user deposits stablecoins and earns yield, you receive **10% of the yield they generate**.

4. **Collect rewards weekly** Referral rewards are distributed weekly and can be withdrawn at any time.


* * *

### **How rewards are calculated**

- Referral rewards are calculated as **10% of the yield generated** by your referred users.

- Rewards are **paid from Ern’s protocol fees**, not deducted from the referred user’s earnings.

- Your referred users always keep **100% of their displayed yield**.


Example:

- A referred user generates $100 in yield

- You receive $10

- The referred user still receives the full $100


* * *

### **Earnings calculator**

The Share & Earn page includes an earnings calculator to help estimate potential rewards based on:

- Number of referred users

- Average deposit size per referral


The calculator is **illustrative only**. Actual earnings depend on:

- Market conditions

- Yield rates on underlying protocols

- Bitcoin price movements

- User behaviour (deposits and withdrawals)


* * *

### **Key details**

- **Referral rate:** 10% of referred users’ yield

- **Paid by:** Protocol fees

- **Distribution:** Weekly

- **Withdrawals:** Anytime

- **Caps:** None


* * *

### **Important notes**

- Referral rewards are variable and not guaranteed.

- Yield rates may change over time.

- Referral rewards are subject to the same on-chain risks as the rest of the protocol.

- Ern reserves the right to update or discontinue the referral programme if required for security, sustainability, or compliance reasons.


This programme does not constitute financial advice or a guarantee of returns.
