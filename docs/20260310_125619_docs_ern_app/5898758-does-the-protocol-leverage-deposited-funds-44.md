No. The strategy is spot and does not rely on leverage, recursive borrowing, or looping positions.

Returns originate solely from lending activity in decentralized money markets and subsequent conversion of the generated yield.

Primary risks therefore include smart contract risk in Ern and the underlying money markets, as well as general stablecoin and liquidity venue risks.
